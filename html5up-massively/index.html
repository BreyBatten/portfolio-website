Started Full-Stack Web Development & Computer Science curriculum part-time at Lambda School

Lambda School is an online, fully remote technical school with a focus on projects in a group setting that mimics the product development cycle and teaches students the skills they need to become Full-Stack developers. Students go through the full development cycle, from product planning and user research to deployment.
